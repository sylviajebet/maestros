Who are we?

A glimpse into who we are, and what we do.

Background

Maestros Casineros is a dynamic dance company specializing in the vibrant art form of Casino style (Cuban salsa) salsa dancing. Founded on the 21st of November, 2016 at Kenyatta University, our company is dedicated to promoting casino style salsa culture, rueda de casino, rueda loca, thrilling choreographies, and providing exceptional dance education. We foster a vibrant community of salsa enthusiasts, with a focus on inclusivity, professionalism, and passion for dance. Maestros Casineros has become a prominent name in the East Africa salsa scene.

Mission

At Maestros Casineros, our mission is to spread the joy of rueda de casino and casino style salsa dancing by providing high-quality instruction, inspiring performances, and vibrant community events. We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where dancers can learn, grow, and express themselves through the art of Cuban salsa. Through our dedication to excellence and passion for the dance, we strive to enrich lives and promote cultural appreciation one salsa step at a time.
